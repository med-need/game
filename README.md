# Pixel Life

Open `index.html` in a browser to watch small animals wander around the screen.
Click anywhere to spawn food; well-fed creatures will reproduce.

Controls at the bottom let you release monsters, summon a savior to hunt them,
and drop random food. When large herds of animals gather they can form a home.
Monsters start with strength 100, growing and showing sharp teeth after each
meal. Saviors begin at 120 strength (maximum size 25 pixels) and may be slain
by stronger monsters. They patrol quietly and eat food when no enemies are
near. If a savior finds a home and the land is peaceful it settles there: the
occupied home generates one food every five seconds and spawns a new animal
every ten seconds. Should a monster or vampire appear, the savior abandons the
home to fight. When two foes collide they each suffer 20–50 damage until one
falls.
