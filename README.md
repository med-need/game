# Pixel Life

Open `index.html` in a browser to watch small animals wander around the screen.
Click anywhere to spawn food; well-fed creatures will reproduce.

Controls at the bottom let you release monsters, summon a savior to hunt them,
and drop random food. When large herds of animals gather they can form a home
that slowly spawns food. Monsters start with strength 100 and grow by 10 for
every victim up to 200. Saviors begin at 120 strength, can eat food to gain 5
power up to 220 and will engage monsters or starving "vampire" animals in
combat. When two foes collide they each suffer 20–50 damage until one falls.
Homes are peaceful buildings that periodically create new food.
